Cards

Used as part of content formats to highlight information. This can be a few words, paragraph or paragraphs with multiple links.

It is important to ask:

  • is the callout neccessary, too many, misused or unneccessary callouts weaken their value
  • which style of callout is relevant, is the information 'useful', 'important' or 'recommending'

Colours can't be relied upon as the only method of communicating the importance and content of a callout. Any callouts should be clearly titled using h2 or h3 tags. Use the appropriate Aria tags for the relevant callouts to ensure screenreaders understand the information contained.

Callout styles

Callout unstyled

This callout does not require an Aria tag but should be labeled with a clear heading.

This style is used for any neutral callouts, where the information needs to be seperated from the content. It should also be used for examples.

<div class="callout">
	<h3>Callout unstyled</h3>
	<p>This callout does not require an Aria tag but should be labeled with a clear heading.</p>
	<p>This style is used for any neutral callouts, where the information needs to be seperated from the content. It should also be used for examples.</p>
</div>
