Revealable

Revealable sections allow you to ‘hide’ content that’s only relevant to some people visiting the page.

The ‘hidden’ content sits under a clickable h3 heading. Once clicked, the rest of the content is revealed.

They’re a great way to make a page easier to read - people can scan through quickly.

Bear in mind that hidden content can be easy for people to miss, so use the revealables sparingly - you should aim to use no more than 4 per page.

Content within the revealables is unlikely to be indexed in search engines. The 'toggle title' - the title inside the revealable - will be, so make sure it’s clear and descriptive.
