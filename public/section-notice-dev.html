Section Notices (development)

Developing a more definitive approach to notice headings, to be clearer for visitors and to avoid distracting from the primary objective of engaging with content.

Section notice types

Survey notice

  • can be used in the discovery phase of epic
  • used in heading of all content areas that could be changing

New notice

  • used on all entirely new content (not amended or updated)
  • automatically removed after 6 weeks
