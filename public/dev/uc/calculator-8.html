Your results

Below is a breakdown of the maximum amount of Universal Credit you could get. You might get less than this - the actual amount you get will depend on where you live and your income. If your claim is successful you'll be told exactly how much you'll get

The maximum amount of Universal Credit you could get

Standard allowance

This is based on your age and whether or not you have a partner.

 £251.77

Housing costs

This part of Universal Credit covers your housing costs - this is the maximum amount someone in your situation can get. The actual amount will depend on where you live, the size of your house, and how many people live there.

Check how much you'll actually get for housing costs on the Directgov website.

 £10.00

Carers

You'll get this if you're a carer.

 £150.39
Total per month £412.16
