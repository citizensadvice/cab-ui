Typography

The font used is Open Sans a sans font chosen for legibility and consistency in online and offline applications. The font comes in various weights, but by default we will be using four of these.

When calling in the font weights or any of the typographic styles, be sure to use the variable rather than the font weight directly.

Open Sans 400 @font-weight-base

Open Sans 600 @font-weight-bold

Open Sans 700 @font-weight-bolder

Open Sans 800 @font-weight-boldest
