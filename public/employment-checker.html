Your result

From what you’ve told us, it looks like you’re self employed.

Outcome 2 SE (maybe)

Your result

From what you’ve told us, there’s a chance that you could be self employed

Your situation will depend on what your contract says though.

It’s worth getting advice from your local Citizens Advice to find out exactly what your rights are.

Outcome 3 WOR

Your result

From what you’ve told us, it looks like you’re a ‘worker’.

This means you’ll have certain employment rights.

You might also be an employee. It’s worth getting advice from your local Citizens Advice to find out exactly what your rights are.

Outcome 4 EM

Your result

From what you’ve told us, it looks like you’re an employee.

This is mainly because you can’t send someone else in to carry out your work.

However, your situation might be more complicated. It’s worth getting advice from your local Citizens Advice.
